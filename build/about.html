About me

My name is Mirella van Teulingen and I'm a Dutch front end developer at Dreamsolution in Delft. Where I have the privilege to work in a team of experts in python, javascript and design.

The key areas of experience include HTML, CSS, Django templating, XML, XSLT and Plone, Zope Page Templates (templating used in Plone). The last few years I'm extending my knowledge and experience with SASS, LESS, jQuery and javascript.

Fronteers

Since 2012 am I a member of Fronteers, a Dutch non-profit trade organisation for front end developers. Since the end of 2013 I'm a volunteer at the workshops committee. We organise workshops about front end techniques, which everyone can attend for a small fee. Check out the Fronteers website to find out what it does for fellow front end developer.

Working as a front end developer never gets bored.
