About me

My name is Mirella van Teulingen, I'm a Dutch front end developer living in a small village called Wateringen. I work in a great team of python and javascript developers at Dreamsolution in Delft.

Blog

This is my personal blog where I write about things I come across while coding and other meetings/conferences I attended. Most articles are related to front end development.

Fronteers

I'm a member and volunteer of Fronteers, a Dutch non-profit trade organisation for front end developers. They organise workshops and meetings for fellow front end developers and once a year the Fronteers Conference event takes place. On my Fronteers page you'll find blog posts of the meetings and conferences I attended.
